---
title: Custom domain
description: Serve your MCP endpoint from your own domain.
---

A custom domain gives your MCP server an address under a domain you control.

## Connect a domain

1. Open your server and go to **Settings**.
2. Find **Custom domain** and enter the full hostname, such as `mcp.example.com`.
3. Copy the DNS record shown by 0mcp into your DNS provider.
4. Return to 0mcp and wait for the status to show that the domain is active.
5. Update your client configuration with the custom endpoint shown in **Setup**.

DNS updates can take time. Keep the record unproxied while ownership and certificate setup are being checked, unless the dashboard tells you otherwise.

Use a hostname only for one server. If setup fails, check for an old record with the same name and make sure you entered a hostname, not a path or full page URL.
